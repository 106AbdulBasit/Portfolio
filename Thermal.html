Thermal Image Face Identification

Home Thermal Image Face Identification

Developed specific sequence of custom filters forincreasing accuracy of face Identification. In our research, we were able to prove that the use of filters or a combination of filters in the pre-processing of data had a positive effect on the model accuracy.
